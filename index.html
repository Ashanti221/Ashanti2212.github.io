ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our company

General Overview

Iziyo Security and Projects Pty Ltd was established in 2014 as a cleaning services organisation and emerged to include Security Services in 2021. 100% black owned company, our core objective is guaranteeing the client's rest of mind while delivering exceptional services.

Company Purpose

Iziyo Security and Projects Pty Ltd is a professional hands-on company whose reputation has been built around raising the industry standard and creating a service catering to each client’s specific needs, from hiring process to the client relationship.

Company Vision

Our vision is to ensure that we become the organisation of choice for areas in and around Stellenbosch through good workmanship, safety, value for money, outstanding customer service and superb ethical conduct.

Company Mission

We create a life-changing experience through the quality of our services. We provide a common ground for all people. Iziyo Security Services is the professional hands-on security company to provide on-site security services to your location.
